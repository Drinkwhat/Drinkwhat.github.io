class app.modules.products.models.ProductMaster(**kwargs)[source]

Bases: Base

Product master data for a specific store.

A ProductMaster represents the canonical product definition used by inventory batches and kitchen recipes. Uniqueness is enforced per store (UNIQUE(name, store_id)).
